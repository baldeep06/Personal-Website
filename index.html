    The train

    The train. Who doesn't love trains? Politicians don't, apparantly. Ever since I was a child, the nearby railroad tracks have always fascinated me. You're telling me thousands of tons of machinery is just whirring down a pair of two steel rods? How cool is that? And the best feeling in the world is when you're on one of these mighty beasts, whirring past traffic. As of late, I've had to make nearly commutes from my University to my home, which has required me to board a cramped bus. When a train, going the same route exists. Why, you might ask? Well, it's because seemingly everyone in this country doesn't care if our trains run at all. I'm getting a bit too animated now. Point is, I like trains.
